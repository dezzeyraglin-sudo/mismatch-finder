THE PROBLEM

Every prop tool says
the same thing.

Overall season stats. Generic "hot" and "cold" labels. No context for why a matchup actually favors the hitter. Most tools miss the signals that move lines.

01
Season averages lie
A hitter batting .420 xwOBA against fastballs may only hit .312 vs RHP fastballs. Public stats don't split by handedness — we do.
02
Starters exit in the 6th
Bullpen throws 40% of every game's PAs. If a hitter crushes the starter's 4-seam but the bullpen throws sliders, you're betting the wrong thing.
03
You don't know the edge
Most tools tell you WHO to play without telling you WHY. When the line moves against you, you can't tell if your thesis is broken or the market is just wrong.
